01 — The Studio

Most décor companies choose from a catalogue. We write the catalogue.

"The hub where vision becomes reality."

Every lantern, console, canopy, and centerpiece you'll see on this page was conceived, fabricated, and finished inside our own workshops — not sourced last-minute from a vendor three calls deep in a supply chain. That distinction sounds small until your event is six days away and a piece needs to change. For us, that's a production note. For a rental company, that's a crisis.

We work across metal, fabric, fiber, and lighting — which means a single brief can move from a brass urli to a hand-pleated fabric ceiling to a rattan pendant lamp, all under one roof, one timeline, and one point of accountability. Planners, hotels, and brands return to us not because we're the loudest name in the room, but because we finish what we start, on the date we said we would.

06 — Questions We're Asked Often
Can you customize an existing design from the catalogue?
Yes — almost everything you see is a starting point. Size, finish, colour, and material can be adjusted, and we regularly combine elements from different pieces into something new.
Do you deliver Pan India and outside India too?
Yes — we deliver globally. We manufacture from our base in India and ship Pan India as standard, with international delivery and installation handled for destination weddings and overseas projects. Tell us the location and date, and we'll confirm logistics and lead time during the consultation.
What's a typical production timeline?
This varies by scope — a few catalogue pieces can move faster than a fully custom installation. We'll give you a realistic timeline at the consultation stage, and we'd rather under-promise than miss your date.
Do you only work on weddings?
No — alongside weddings and destination weddings, we regularly produce décor and props for corporate events, hotel and hospitality refreshes, brand activations, festivals, exhibitions, and private celebrations.
Can you support destination weddings outside the venue's home city or country?
Yes — destination weddings, in India and abroad, are a regular part of our work. We plan logistics, transport, and on-site installation as part of the project timeline from the start.
Will your team be on-site for installation, or do we assemble it ourselves?
Our team installs on-site as standard. We don't consider a project complete until it's built, checked, and matches the approved design in person.
